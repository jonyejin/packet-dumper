#  Exponential Decay vs Power-law Decay
When $x$ is time, 

Exponential Decay: $f(x) ∝ e^{-x}$
Power-Law Decay: $f(x) ∝ x^{-2}$

- Power-Law Decay is slowly decreasing than Exponential Decay.
